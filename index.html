Inhabit - Social Habit Tracker

Inhabit is a beautiful, cross-platform social habit tracking app. It utilises Flutter and Firebase to allow users to keep eachother accountable with groups, chats and notifications.

This was a project I did for myself and some friends to track our progress through a mental toughness challenge, and I decided to polish it up and make it accessible to everyone.

Float - Get Essays Done

Float encourages students to complete a set number of words in their essays each day, and allows them to track how far behind (or ahead!) they might be falling.

This is one of the earliest projects I did as a freelancer back in 2020 and went on to be featured as Apple's App of the Day.

PitcheroGPS

PitcheroGPS allows grassroots sports clubs access to deep analytical data on player performance via a bespoke GPS tracker and detailed app.

I worked at Pitchero from 2022-2023 and was the sole developer on the app side of the PitcheroGPS project. This work included interfacing with the custom GPS tracker and firmware via Bluetooth Low Energy and converting the data from low level binary to usable JSON, fed back up to the API.
